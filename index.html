SmartFollow is a student's evaluation and monitoring software that allows to follow students on an every-day basis, not only via their grades, but also using customizable criteria that are then analyzed by an artificial intelligence in order to identify students that might be in trouble before it is too late.

Project presentation

The goal of the project is to offer a complete solution for school organization and student monitoring that is entirely customizable to fit the school. It provides a layer of artificial intelligence that analyzes the changes in each student's schooling and detects potential issues before it has too big of an impact on the student.

Our solution is aimed towards 4 main user categories:

  • The administrators of the software will configure the software, by defining the evaluation criteria, business workflows, user groups, alerts and graphs to be displayed on the students profiles, ...
  • The school administration will be able to manage rooms reservations, classes planning, student's absences, ...
  • The teachers can fill the evaluation criteria during each lesson, with, for example, the behavior of a student (be it positive or negative) or the exame grades.
  • The students and their parents have access to a personalized page summarizing their schooling by displaying graphs and alerts showing the progress in each class. For example the software can generate an alert congratulating the student if he made a good progress in a specific class or in his behavior.

Current softwares are focusing on administrative school management, without providing solutions for schooling monitoring and without allowing the administrators to customize the solution to fit their specific needs. Moreover, existing solutions offer fewer possibilities and force the schools to use multiple softwares and pay for multiple licenses to manage all the aspects of their organization.

SmartFollow allows to completely manage the structure so that all the functionalities are available in a single tool, be it for teachers, the school administration, the students and their parents.
